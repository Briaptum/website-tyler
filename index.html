welcome to louisville!

We are a registered student organization at the University of Louisville. If you are in need of arrival assistance such as an airport pickup or temporary housing, sign up below. All of our services are FREE!

New student? Need help?

Arrival Assistance

Click on the link below and fill out the form to receive arrival assistance. You will be contacted by a student or volunteer soon to confirm your details.

Temporary Housing

Will your flight arrive after business hours in Louisville? Free temporary housing is available for up to 4 days while you get checked into your permanent apartment.

American Friends & Family

Want to be matched with an American volunteer/family who has lived in another country? They understand what it is like to move across the world and they want to help you acclimate to Louisville and the university. Fill out the form below to be matched to an American friend or family.
